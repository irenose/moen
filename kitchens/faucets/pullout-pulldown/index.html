Pullout / Pulldown Faucets

Pullout and pulldown faucets offer flexibility and ease of use by combining side sprayer and standard faucet functionality in one unit. All pullout models and select pulldown models are equipped with Moen Reflex® technology so they provide smooth operation, self-retract and securely dock.
