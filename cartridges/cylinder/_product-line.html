1200 Cylinder Cartridge

For One-Handle Kitchen & Bath Faucets

The original Moen cartridge, the 1200 provided one-handle operation along with a choice of tempered water. It was used in all one-handle faucets, except the Posi-Temp® shower valve, prior to the introduction of the 1225 in 1981.

How it is used:

Currently used as a replacement cartridge for older faucets. It is not compatible with newer faucets, which are now designed around the Duralast® cartridge.

Construction:

Brass construction; 180° rotation from hot to cold.

Quick Fix:

No repair kit available. Replace with 1225 unless application is Moentrol 3 function vertical spa transfer valve—then replace with Moen part #116719 cartridge. Note that the 1225 offers superior function for passing line trash.

Benefits:

  • Accommodates back-to-back installation & supports hot/cold reversibility.
  • Volume control with temperature memory.
  • Fits older one-handle faucets.
  • Replaced by 1225 cartridge.

1222 Cylinder Cartridge

For One-Handle Posi-Temp Faucets

Higher flow-rate valve for tub & shower applications.

How it is used:

Fits all Posi-Temp® tub/shower faucets.

Construction:

Stainless steel and brass construction with a polysulfone shell to resist corrosion. Cycling valve 270° rotation.

Quick Fix:

Repair or replacement fixes most faucet problems. Repair kit available for warranty issues (Moen part# 98040).

Benefits:

  • Accommodates back-to-back installation, tub and shower flow reversibility.
  • Built-in pressure-balancing mechanism maintains temperature +/-3° during pressure drop with showerhead flow rates as low as 1.5gpm.
Troubleshooting: 1222 Cylinder Cartridge

1224 Cylinder Cartridge

For Two-Handle and Three-Handle Kitchen & Bath Faucets

Flexible use high flow-rate cartridge.

How it is used:

Prior to the introduction of Duralast® two-handle cartridges, the 1224 was designed to support all Moen two- and three-handle faucets, including pot fillers as well as non-pressure balancing tub and shower faucets.

Construction:

Stainless steel construction, Acetal shell for reduced corrosion, 180° rotation for hot and cold. Designed to allow major line trash to pass through during installation.

Quick Fix:

Replace cartridge; there are no parts offered to repair the cartridge.

Benefits:

  • Interchangeable between hot and cold.
  • Simple on/off volume control.
Troubleshooting: 1224 Cylinder Cartridge

1225 Cylinder Cartridge

For One-Handle Faucets

Introduced in 1981, the patented 1225 Magnum non-metallic cartridge is impervious to corrosion and mineral build-up and is designed to allow major line trash to pass through.

How it is used:

Fits Moen one-handle kitchen faucets launched prior to 2009 and Moen one-handle lavatory faucets launched prior to 2011. Fits all one-handle non-pressure balanced tub/shower faucets. Has temperature memory setting and volume control.

Construction:

Stainless steel, brass and copper construction, polysulfone shell, 180° rotation from hot to cold.

Quick Fix:

Repair or replacement fixes most faucet pr1200 kit available for warranty issues (Moen part# 92451).

Benefits:

  • Accommodates back-to-back installation and supports hot/cold reversibility.
  • Retrofits 1200 cartridge.
  • Includes volume control & temperature memory.
Troubleshooting: 1225 Cylinder Cartridge

1248 Cylinder Cartridge

For Two-Handle Roman Tub Faucets

High flow-rate for shower and tub applications.

How it is used:

Designed to fit all Moen® two-handle Roman tub faucets, and ½” ExactTemp® thermostatic valves. Engineered to have a larger outlet port for higher flow rate.

Construction:

Stainless steel construction, Acetal shell, 180° rotation for hot and cold. Designed to allow major line trash to pass through during installation. Delivers 15 to 17 gallons per minute at 60 psi.

Quick Fix:

Replace cartridge; there are no parts offered to repair the cartridge.

Benefits:

  • Universal design supports multiple valves and handle configurations.
  • Interchangeable between hot and cold.
  • Simple on/off.
  • Volume control.
Troubleshooting: 1248 Cylinder Cartridge
