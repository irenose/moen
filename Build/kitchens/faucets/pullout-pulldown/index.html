Pullout / Pulldown Faucets

Pullout and pulldown faucets offer flexibility and ease of use by combining side sprayer and standard faucet functionality in one unit. All pullout models and select pulldown models are equipped with Moen Reflex® technology so they provide smooth operation, self-retract and securely dock.

Product Overview

Pullout Faucets

Pullout faucets feature a wand that users pull out from the faucet base to easily direct the flow of water. The wand integrates faucet and sprayer nozzles. All of these faucets are handle clocked to prevent backsplash crashing.

Installation Instructions

Pulldown Faucets

Pulldown faucets are similar to pullout faucets. However only the faucet head pulls away from faucet body for maximum control of water stream. All of these faucets are one-handle. Select models feature MotionSense™ with two electronic sensors for hands-free operation.
