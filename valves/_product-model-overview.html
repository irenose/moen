Overview of Moen Valve Models

ioDigital™ Vertical Spa Valve

Digital Thermostatic with computer-controlled temperature and flow.

ExactTemp® ¾” Thermostatic Valve

Fits all Posi-Temp faucets. Resists corrosion.

Pressure-balancing volume control valve.
