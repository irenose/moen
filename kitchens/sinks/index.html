Sinks

Moen offers a broad range of drop-in and undermount sinks to complement our extensive selection of faucet products. Choices include single, double-equal or double-offset bowl options and styles range from zero radius to D-Shaped bowls and even low-middle divide selections. All Moen sinks feature SoundSHIELD™ technology ensuring exceptional noise reduction and quiet dependability.

This module will help you understand the different types of sinks, important planning considerations and the product lines offered by Moen.
