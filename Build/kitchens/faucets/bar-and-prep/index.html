Bar & Prep Faucets

A variety of specialty service faucets are available for added food and beverage prep options. The two most prominent types are pot-filler faucets and bar faucets. Moen offers both types to complement many of our most popular faucet styles.

Product Overview

Pot Fillers

Pot fillers are mounted above stoves and feature a long pendulum arm that can be aligned directly above large pots for easy filling.

Bar / Beverage Faucets

Bar/beverage faucets are designed to fit small bar, butler or other service areas where light food or beverage preparation takes place. These are available in one- and two-handle configurations. An optional filtration system is also available.
