Duralast® Cartridges

Engineered from the latest Moen ceramic disc technology, with a stainless steel stem and with fewer parts, Duralast® cartridges enable more compact, sleeker faucet styles prevalent in today's market. The cartridges are used in one-handle faucet, two-handle kitchen and bath faucets, and higher-flow applications such as Roman tubs or pot fillers.

Product Overview

Duralast Benefits

Duralast cartridges are manufactured to the highest standards and offer significant benefits including:

  • a consistently smooth handle feel for the life of the faucet with precise control of water for the life of the faucet
  • improved stop strength
  • ability to withstand use and abuse, even under hard-water conditions

Overview of Moen Duralast® Models

1234

The new 1234 cartridge is now available on all two-handle widespread faucets and is moving into select two-handle centerset faucets.

1258

The 1258 is used on two-handle tub/showers, Roman tubs, pot fillers and floor-mount tub fillers.

1255

The 1255 is used on one-handle lavatory and kitchen faucets.

Designed for the trade

Easy to install

Simple product design makes Duralast® easy to install. It slips easily and correctly into place with no guesswork, thanks to a keyed outer shell. Consistent handle feel is not affected by the torque of the cartridge nut, which cannot be over tightened.

Bonus: All one-handle faucets with Duralast cartridges include pre-installed ⅜” flexible supply lines.

1234 Duralast cartridge

For Two-Handle Kitchen & Bath Faucets

The new 1234 applies Duralast technology to two-handle faucets.

How it is used:

Select two-handle kitchen and bath faucets and Sip™ one-handle beverage/filtration faucets. The Eva and Brantford product lines now use 1234; and Glyde and Dartmoor are two new faucets using 1234 in 2016.

Construction:

Stainless steel stem and ceramic disc, Acetal shell, 90° rotation for hot and cold.

Benefits:

  • Interchangeable between hot and cold.
  • Volume control.
  • Keyed shell for positive installation confirmation.

1258 Duralast Cartridge

For Two-Handle Tub Showers, Roman Tubs, Pot Fillers & Floor-Mount Tub Fillers

Duralast technology for tub, shower and other high flow-rate applications.

How it is used:

High flow-rate applications such as Roman tub, two-handle tub/showers, two-handle floor-mounted tub fillers, and pot fillers.

Construction:

Stainless steel stem and ceramic disc, Acetal shell, 90° rotation for hot and cold.

Benefits:

  • Interchangeable between hot and cold.
  • Volume control.
  • Keyed shell for positive installation confirmation.

1255 Duralast Cartridge

For One-Handle Kitchen & Bath Faucets

Moen’s newest one-handle faucet cartridge.

How it is used:

The 1255 Duralast cartridge has replaced all 1200 and 1225 cartridges within one-handle lavatory and kitchen faucets starting in 2009, with the transition complete in 2010. The 1200 & 1225 are for models manufactured prior to that timeframe.

Construction:

Stainless steel stem and ceramic disc, durable plastic shell, 100° rotation from hot to cold.

Benefits:

  • Volume control and temperature mix.
  • Keyed shell for positive installation confirmation.
